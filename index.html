What is Cashout

Cashout is a flexible decentralized Blockchain application that allows users to save money in groups using the Near token for a specified period of months after which those money will be disbursed to individual account according to the amount a person invest or saved.
Token can invested at any given time before the disbursment date and can not also be withdrawn until the expected month.
The Cashout app stores every transaction on the BlockChain thereby making it difficult to be tempared with.
